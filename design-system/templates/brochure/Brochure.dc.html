Group · A pioneering story

Founded to serve U.S. clients from Switzerland

After the global financial crisis of 2008, the situation became very difficult for U.S. clients worldwide. That was the moment when Francesco Bernasconi and Siro Spellini embarked on a journey to provide Swiss-quality global investment services exclusively to North American clients.

In 2009 they jointly founded LFA — Lugano Financial Advisors S.A. Their pioneering work led LFA to become one of the first Swiss companies registered with the SEC, in 2010.

LFA's long-term relationships with Swiss custodian banks ensure clients continuous access to, and security of, their assets. Dedicated Swiss-quality service and an unconditional compliance culture contributed to our success.

A decade of growth, innovation and strong partnerships has strengthened our reputation — contributing to Switzerland's prominent position in cross-border wealth management.

LFA Investment Process

Our clients take the center stage

We, at LFA, believe that our clients — and the relationship we hold with them — are the most valuable assets we own. Our aim is to make them feel more like partners than clients. For this reason, our investment process is built and continuously upgraded to improve the service and experience delivered to them.

Our success is based on the ability to customise our products and services, tailoring them to our partners' unique goals, concerns and values. In time, through our proximity, we aim to become a safe harbor in our clients' minds.

Our decision-making process combines quantitative and qualitative analysis. We embrace technological innovation, yet remain strong supporters of the human touch. We invest while striking the proper balance between passive investments and active managers — keeping a close eye on costs, because markets can be volatile, but costs and taxes are forever.

It is the fact that we invest with prudence and care that portrays LFA as a safe harbor. These principles will always be at the heart of our process.
