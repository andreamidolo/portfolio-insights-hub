**Standard LFA section opener:** rose eyebrow, italic-serif headline (often a question), optional lead paragraph.

```jsx
<SectionHeading
  eyebrow="Why LFA"
  title="Why would a U.S. investor work with a Swiss-based firm?"
  lead="LFA is headquartered in Switzerland and works exclusively with U.S. investors…"
  align="center"
/>
```

`tone="white"` on navy/photo bands; `italic={false}` for roman headlines.
